How Breathing Detection Works

Technology Used:

  • Pressure Sensors: Under-mattress load cells detect weight distribution changes
  • Ballistocardiography (BCG): Measures micro-movements from heartbeat and breathing
  • Piezoelectric Sensors: Convert body movements into electrical signals
  • AI Processing: Algorithms filter noise and extract breathing patterns

How It Works:

  1. Sensors detect chest/body movement as you breathe
  2. Each breath causes slight pressure changes on the mattress
  3. Signal processing filters out other movements
  4. AI counts breathing cycles per minute
  5. Alerts trigger if rate is abnormal (<12 or >20 breaths/min)

Privacy: No cameras or audio - only pressure/movement data
